About Me :

I am currently training at BeCode to become a junior web developer full stack. I am ambitious and always looking for a new challenge. When I was younger, I was not a very good student. I think it's because I couldn't find anything that interested me, then I discovered web development. 4 months ago, I was a simple forklift operator who went to work without desire, without passion. I wanted to try a professional reconversion in computer science, and I don't regret anything! I'm a very motivated person and I always want to know more and learn more. There is a saying of Charles-Augustin Sainte-Beuve which defines me quite well:

Si vous n’essayez jamais, vous ne réussirez jamais, mais si vous essayez, vous risquez de vous étonner vous-même
